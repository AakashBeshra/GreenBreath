About GreenBreath

GreenBreath is designed to reduce carbon emissions using advanced **photocatalytic technology**. Our mission is to create a sustainable and healthier future.

Our Technology

Using **Titanium Dioxide (TiO₂) and UV light**, GreenBreath efficiently separates CO₂ into carbon and oxygen, ensuring an eco-friendly air purification process.

Environmental Impact

Our system helps **combat climate change** by reducing carbon footprints and improving air quality worldwide.
